Risk Assessment
Fund Risk
The collection of funds is on the less risky end of the spectrum. The primary driver is their strong Revere scores and diverse portfolios.
How it's measured
While all venture is inherently risky, things that could make some funds more risky than others is the industry, the number of investments, and qualitative attributes measured by Revere.
Return Assessment
Fund Return
The collect Total Addressable Market for the collection of funds is $26B. This is a large market for the funds to capitalize on. This means that if they are able to capitalize on the market there will be significant returns. The largest TAM by sector in this group is Enterprise at $15B.
Track Record
Track Record
The funds represent the 78th percentile of all funds on the Revere platform in terms of total return of prior track records.
How it's measured
Based off of 122 individual track records across 2008 to 2022 vintages and includes a combination of form funds and individuals' angel track records.
Transparency
Transparency
The overall transparency of the funds is excellent. This means that they have lots of data room items available that the most demanding institutional investors would want. It also means that they do a good job of sending out updates.
