Results Overview
Your overall portfolio is considered balanced.

Our algorithm suggests a more moderately diversified portfolio that combines emerging and established managers. Asset allocation includes exposure to fast-growing industry sectors and more early-stage companies where valuations are attractive.

Concerning fund manager attributes, we select managers with at least two areas of differentiation and will consider first-time fund managers.
Risk Assessment
Fund Risk
Funds in your portfolio will include the most seasoned managers. First-time funds may only be considered if led by experienced managers with established track records.
Return Assessment
Fund Return
Your portfolio includes exposure to promising industry sectors with well-quantified market sizes. Most startups operating in this dynamic utilize technology to drive better unit economics and go-to-market tactics to win market share. Fund returns will be driven by building exposure to many category winners who can scale quickly.
Track Record
Track Record
The funds represent the 78th percentile of all funds on the Revere platform in terms of total return of prior track records.
How it's measured
Our measurement is based on 122 individual track records across 2008 to 2022 vintages and includes a combination of form funds and individuals' angel track records.
Transparency
Transparency
We identify investible fund managers as those with at least a 90% score on the Verified checklist.
How it's measured
Our measurement is based on Revere's Verified data checklist, which includes 30 items covering everything from data room materials, service provider names, and portfolio models.
Revere Category Analaysis
Positive Attributes
Overall the collection of funds scores very highly across Team and Sourcing. These are two important categories because a strong team is important and because where a team sources their deals from is important.
Attributes to keep on watch
The fund terms and Operating experience are only industry standard which means they are about the level at which we would expect for an emerging fund manager.
